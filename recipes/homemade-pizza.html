Pizza

How to make it

This homemade pizza is the best ever!

You wont believe the taste!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Olive oil
  • Water
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozarella
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Mix Flour,Yeast,Olive Oil and water into a dough and let it rise.
  2. Preheat oven to 400C.
  3. After dough has risen knead it into a flat pizza shape.
  4. Take som Tomato sauce and put it on the dough.
  5. Put cheese on the pizza.
  6. Put the pizza in the oven for 5 minutes or until the bread is cooked.
  7. Finish with basil after the oven.
  8. Done!
